About ReceiptKeep

🔒 100% Private

This app uses Tesseract.js to process images directly on your device. No images or data are ever sent to a server.

💡 Tips for Success

  • Use bright, even lighting.
  • Keep the receipt flat and steady.
  • The app looks for the largest currency value as the "Total".
